About



Hi, I'm Tiffany.
I design and code digital and spatial experiences.

I am a senior at Carnegie Mellon University studying Information Systems and Human-Computer Interaction. My interest in development and design is driven by the fact that together, they are powerful tools to serve and empower people. I apply my understanding of visual and interactive communication within the context of technology to create delightful and empathetic experiences. I have incredible optimism for technology’s ability to enrich lives, and I'm always exploring the possibilities.

In my free time, you might find me dabbling with new technologies, taking photos, baking sweets, or fawning over cute dogs.
